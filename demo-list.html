üì± How to Use
  1. Load the extension in Chrome (chrome://extensions/)
  2. Navigate to any product page or enter a URL
  3. Click "Get Title & Price" or "Get Current Tab Info"
  4. If valid product info is found, click "Add to List"
  5. Click the product count badge to show/hide your saved list
  6. Use "Visit" to open products or "Remove" to delete them
  7. Export your list or clear all products as needed
